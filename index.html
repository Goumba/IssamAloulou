Project Holy Mushroom Dude

A mushroom battle priest who lives in a swamp, defending his homeland. My friend showed me his little animation and it gave me the inspiration to try a character with textures more realistic than what I previously worked on.
7042 tris
Inspired by Mushroom Dude: https://sta.sh/02g7w4vywrnn

Project Big Bad Robot

An old rusty robot that used to be the fondation's first line of defense. Victim of technology's advance, his new home's the scrapyard. This project was born during my school session, so it took a little longer than expected. I wanted to try something more hard edged and realistic to diversify my portfolio and try new things! I didn't follow someone else's concept art, I just figured what would be nice along the way.
This asset is not meant for video games.

Pack It Up!

This is the result of my first game development project. Pack it Up is a battle royal type game where you have to gather material to build a weapon and defeat your enemies. I worked on the house(and what's inside), the poster, the rocky formation, the small pond and the grass. I also modeled the game's character.

Project Turning Into a Beast

A fantastic beast a friend of mine created in 2D that I've decided to adapt for 3D games. I wanted to explore new tools, like Zbrush and Substance Painter, to help me in my projects and I love his drawings.
